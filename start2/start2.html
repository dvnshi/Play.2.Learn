RIGHTS TO E D U C A T I O N
The Prohibition of Child Marriage Act, 2006, is a law in India that aims to prevent child marriages. Its preamble states that the act is enacted to prohibit child marriages and address related matters to safeguard the rights and well-being of children in India.

The Prohibition of Child Marriage Act is like a rule in India that says children should not get married before they are adults. It's important to protect kids from getting married too early because it can affect their health and future. This law helps make sure children can grow up and make their own choices when they are older.
